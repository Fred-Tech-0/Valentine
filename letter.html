To my most favorite person,

As I sit here writing this, I am overwhelmed by the journey we have shared. It is not often that two souls find a connection that feels as effortless and profound as ours. From the moment we met, my world began to shift in the most beautiful ways...

You are my constant, my calm in the middle of a storm, and the person I want to wake up to every single morning. I love the way we laugh at things no one else understands. I love how you are committed to me and how you support me...

Thank you for choosing me. I love you more than yesterday, but less than I will tomorrow.

Always Yours.
